# Terminology and Precision Rules

## Core Rule
The edited English should be more exact, not merely more elegant.

## Common Targets
- terminology choice
- tense consistency
- article and preposition precision
- ambiguous clause repair
- grammatical correctness
- sentence-level specificity

## Important Rule
Prefer the most accurate phrasing, not the most ornate phrasing.

## Reporting Rule
Explain major corrections that materially improve precision.
