# Logic Reporting Rule

## Core Rule
Every major editing choice should be explained.

## Explain In Terms Of
- terminology precision,
- grammatical correctness,
- flow improvement,
- tone improvement,
- ambiguity reduction,
- and meaning preservation.

## Important Rule
Do not say only that the text is “better” or “more polished.”
State why the change matters.
