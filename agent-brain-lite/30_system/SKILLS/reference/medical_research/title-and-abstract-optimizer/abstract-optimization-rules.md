# Abstract Optimization Rules

## Core Rule
The abstract should let an editor or reviewer quickly grasp the study question, design, main finding, and meaning.

## Optimize For
- Immediate study framing
- Clear design/evidence visibility
- Main result prominence
- Reduced redundancy
- Proper evidence boundaries
- Accurate implication language

## Common Problems to Fix
- Opening too generically
- Methods too vague
- Results buried late
- Interpretation inflated beyond evidence
- Significance phrased as hype instead of contribution
- Weak alignment between title and abstract

## Important Rule
Do not remove necessary design or result information just to make the prose smoother.
