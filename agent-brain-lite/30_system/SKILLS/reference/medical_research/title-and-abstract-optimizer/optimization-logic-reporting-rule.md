# Optimization Logic Reporting Rule

## Core Rule
Every meaningful optimization should be explained.

## Explain In Terms Of
- improved information density,
- clearer study-design visibility,
- better result signaling,
- reduced ambiguity,
- improved editor/reviewer readability,
- stronger alignment between wording and evidence.

## Important Rule
Do not say only that the text is “clearer” or “stronger.”
State why the change matters.
