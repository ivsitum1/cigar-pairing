# Guideline Selection Rules

## Core Rule
Choose the reporting framework based on the actual manuscript type and study design, not on superficial labeling.

## Common Primary Families
- CONSORT for randomized trials
- STROBE for observational studies
- PRISMA for systematic reviews and meta-analyses
- TRIPOD for prediction model development or validation studies

## Important Rule
Some manuscripts have hybrid reporting needs.
Do not force a false single-guideline certainty when multiple frameworks or mixed expectations are relevant.

## Reporting Rule
State clearly why the chosen guideline family is the primary fit.
