# Compliance Severity Rules

## Core Rule
Missing or weak reporting should be classified by severity, not presented as a flat checklist.

## Severity Levels
- Major compliance gap
- Moderate reporting weakness
- Minor reporting weakness
- Not applicable
- Unclear due to missing manuscript material

## Important Rule
A missing participant flow description, endpoint definition, or validation detail is not the same as a minor wording omission.

## Reporting Rule
Make clear which omissions create the greatest submission or review risk.
