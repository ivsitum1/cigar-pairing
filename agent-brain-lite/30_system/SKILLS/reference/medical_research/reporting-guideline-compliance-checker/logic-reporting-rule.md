# Logic Reporting Rule

## Core Rule
Every major compliance judgment should be explained.

## Explain In Terms Of
- transparency risk,
- interpretability risk,
- reviewer confusion risk,
- desk-rejection vulnerability,
- and correction priority.

## Important Rule
Do not say only that an item is “missing.”
State why the omission matters.
