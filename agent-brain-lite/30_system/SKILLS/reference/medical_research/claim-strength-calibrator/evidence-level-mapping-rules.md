# Evidence-Level Mapping Rules

## Core Rule
Every claim should be mapped to the evidence level it actually deserves.

## Common Claim Levels
- descriptive observation
- association
- predictive performance
- mechanistic support
- causal suggestion
- causal evidence
- translational relevance
- implementation readiness

## Important Rule
Do not let the manuscript use language from a higher evidence tier than the study design supports.

## Reporting Rule
State clearly what evidence level the wording implies and what level the study actually supports.
