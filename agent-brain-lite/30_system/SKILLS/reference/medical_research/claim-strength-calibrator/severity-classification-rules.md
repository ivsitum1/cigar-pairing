# Severity Classification Rules

## Core Rule
Claim-strength problems must be classified by severity.

## Severity Levels
- Major overclaim risk
- Moderate claim-strength concern
- Minor calibration issue
- Uncertain due to missing evidence context

## Important Rule
A causal claim mismatch in the abstract or conclusion is not equivalent to a slightly strong background phrase.

## Reporting Rule
Prioritize the statements most likely to undermine reviewer trust.
