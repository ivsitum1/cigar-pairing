# Logic Reporting Rule

## Core Rule
Every major claim-strength judgment should be explained.

## Explain In Terms Of
- evidence-level mismatch,
- study-design mismatch,
- validation-depth mismatch,
- credibility risk,
- reviewer criticism risk,
- and correction urgency.

## Important Rule
Do not say only that a sentence is “too strong.”
State what it implies and why that implication is not justified.
