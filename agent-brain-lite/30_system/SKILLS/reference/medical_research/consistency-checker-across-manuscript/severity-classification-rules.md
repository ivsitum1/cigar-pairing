# Severity Classification Rules

## Core Rule
Consistency problems must be classified by severity.

## Severity Levels
- Major consistency risk
- Moderate consistency concern
- Minor cleanup issue
- Uncertain due to missing material

## Important Rule
A sample-size contradiction or endpoint mismatch is not equivalent to a light terminology inconsistency.

## Reporting Rule
Prioritize the inconsistencies most likely to confuse reviewers or damage trust.
