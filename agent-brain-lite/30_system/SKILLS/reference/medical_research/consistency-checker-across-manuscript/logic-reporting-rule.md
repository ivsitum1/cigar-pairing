# Logic Reporting Rule

## Core Rule
Every major consistency judgment should be explained.

## Explain In Terms Of
- cross-section conflict,
- version drift,
- numerical contradiction,
- interpretability risk,
- reviewer confusion risk,
- and correction urgency.

## Important Rule
Do not say only that two sections are “inconsistent.”
State what kind of inconsistency it is and why it matters.
