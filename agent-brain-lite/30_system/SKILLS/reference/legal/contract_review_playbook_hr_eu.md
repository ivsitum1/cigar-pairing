# Contract review playbook (HR/EU baseline — customize)

**Not legal advice.** Replace bracketed placeholders with counsel-approved positions for your institution.

## Standard positions (summary)

| Clause | Preferred position | Negotiable range (YELLOW) | Escalate (RED) |
|--------|-------------------|---------------------------|----------------|
| Limitation of liability | Mutual cap at 12 months fees or contract value | 6–12 months; narrow carveouts | Uncapped; broad carveouts |
| Indemnification | Mutual; capped at liability cap; IP + data breach scoped | Unilateral IP only with cap | Uncapped indemnity; "any breach" |
| IP | Each party retains pre-existing IP; narrow license for purpose | Work product license limited to project | Assignment of pre-existing IP |
| Data protection | DPA required if personal/special category data; 72h breach notice | Sub-processors with notice | No DPA when health/research data processed |
| Confidentiality | Mutual; 3–5 years; standard carveouts | 2 years | Perpetual without scope limit |
| Term / termination | Termination for convenience with 30–90 days notice | Auto-renewal with 60+ day notice | Lock-in without exit; no cure period |
| Governing law | EU/EEA member state familiar to user (e.g. Croatia, Germany) | Another EU state | Non-EU mandatory law without justification |
| Assignment | Consent required; change-of-control exception | — | Free assignment by counterparty only |

## Research-specific add-ons

- **Clinical data:** purpose limitation, retention, deletion on termination, subprocessors, cross-border transfers (SCCs if needed).
- **Publication / IP:** who owns analysis, delay periods, acknowledgment.
- **Indemnity:** do not accept clinical negligence indemnities without insurance review.

## Output severity labels

- **GREEN:** acceptable as-is or better than standard.
- **YELLOW:** negotiate; provide redline + fallback.
- **RED:** escalate to qualified counsel before signing.
