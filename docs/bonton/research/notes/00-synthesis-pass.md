# Notes — first synthesis pass (2026-07-16)

## Continuities useful for our book

1. **Consent before flame** — Victorian/Edwardian manuals and modern lounge guides agree: ask, accept no, do not negotiate.
2. **Smoke as shared air** — historical texts obsess over breath/clothes/rooms; modern guides obsess over direction of exhale, phones, and ash.
3. **Host obligations** — offer a cigar when you smoke; change coat / rinse mouth historically; tip and buy on-site today.
4. **Separate spaces** — smoking room as gendered architecture (Country Life, Baltzer-Lovato) → today: lounge vs street vs home balcony.
5. **Ritual vs vanity** — Davidoff-style rules (half smoke, no mid-cigar light) vs our hospitality focus (presence, measure, leave cleaner).

## Caution

- Many 19th-c sources encode sexism and class hierarchy. Use as **historical colour**, not as norms to revive.
- Lounge blogs are commercial; extract norms, verify against multiple sources.
- Bridges is copyrighted; borrow **structure** (short precepts), write original rules.
